How it works

Four steps to not being alone

Designed to be frictionless. From posting to meeting in under 5 minutes.

1
Post what you're doing
Name the activity, place, time, and how many people you want. Keep it casual — this isn't an event, it's a plan.
"heading to Third Wave in 20, anyone want to come?"
2
Nearby people discover it
Your plan shows up in the feed and on the map for people within range who match your activity type. Time-sensitive — older plans fade out automatically.
3
They request to join
You see their profile, interests, and vouch rating. Accept and a temporary chat opens. No acceptance, no contact — you're always in control.
No cold messages. Ever.
4
Meet up. Vouch each other.
After the hangout, both people can rate and vouch for each other. Vouches build your trust score — the more you have, the more visible your plans become.
Built-in trust

Safety isn't a feature.
It's the foundation.

Meeting strangers is only comfortable when the system makes it safe. Every design decision on cltr. is made with that in mind.

🔒
Request-only contact
Nobody can message you unless you accept their join request. Zero cold DMs, always.
⭐
Vouch ratings
Every hangout builds your reputation. Ratings are mutual — both people vouch, or neither does.
✅
Verified profiles
Phone + social verification before you can post or join plans. Real people only.
👥
You control the guest list
See who's requesting, check their profile and vouches, then decide. Your plan, your call.
